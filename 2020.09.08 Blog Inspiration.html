Inspiration can come at any time

It took me a while to begin learning Finnish language. At first I just arrived for an internship, three months, then constantly prolonged fellowship… Then I began my PhD and planned to leave Finland when I graduate so during all that time I didn’t see a point to learn one of the most difficult languages. I was content, many people spoke English, Google auto translated all their pages, almost all friends were international and spoke English. Definitely no need to know the fourth language. Consequently, I graduated, many things can change in four years.

In four years I found friends here, I began liking the country, weather was too similar to Lithuanian to be bothered by it too much too. Finland slowly creped into my heart without me noticing it. So I decided to try and stay, especially that government’s social support was guaranteed for a year after graduation. Long story short, it didn’t go smoothly! After many attempts to find a job in local industry I realized that if I want to stay here I have to try harder and begin learning a hard language. I began attending Finnish language courses and apparently doodle earrings on the pages of the printed exercise pages. Inspiration really can come at any time, maybe my brain really needed a minute or two to chill with some creativity and then return to the world of strict rules. Who knows. I did not use any of these yet, bur I am excited to think them someday soon. Stay tuned!
